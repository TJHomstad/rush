How to Play Glaze Rush

Goal

Rotate the pipe tiles to connect the sprinkle source (diamond) to every donut on the board. When sprinkles flow to all donuts, you win!

Controls

Click / Tap Rotate tile 90° clockwise
Ctrl+Click Rotate tile counter-clockwise
Right-click Lock/unlock a tile in place
Long press (mobile) Lock/unlock a tile

Tile Types

  • Straight — connects two opposite sides
  • Elbow — connects two adjacent sides (90° bend)
  • T-Junction — connects three sides
  • Cross — connects all four sides
  • Donut (terminal) — end-point with one connection

Rules

  • All pipes must form a single connected network
  • Sprinkles must reach every donut from the source
  • No closed loops allowed in the network
  • Each puzzle has exactly one solution

Tips

  • Start with corner and edge tiles — they have fewer valid orientations
  • Lock tiles you're confident about to avoid accidentally rotating them
  • Watch the flow animation — connected pipes turn chocolate brown
  • Use Undo if you make a mistake

Difficulty Levels

  • Glazed (Easy) — 5×5 and 7×7 grids, mostly straights and elbows
  • Frosted (Medium) — 7×7 and 10×10 grids, mix of all tile types
  • Sprinkled (Hard) — 15×15 and 20×20 grids, complex networks
  • Double Chocolate (Expert) — 25×25 grids, maximum complexity
