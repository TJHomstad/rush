Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 25, 2026

What We Collect

Glaze Rush is a family puzzle game. We collect minimal data:

  • First name — used for leaderboard display
  • Puzzle completion times — for leaderboard rankings
  • Session token — stored in your browser's localStorage for login persistence

What We Don't Collect

  • No email addresses
  • No tracking or analytics
  • No cookies (we use localStorage)
  • No third-party scripts

Data Storage

Your game progress is stored locally in your browser. Scores submitted to leaderboards are stored on our server.

Guest Play

You can play without logging in. Guest progress is stored locally only and cannot be submitted to leaderboards.

Contact

Questions? This is a family game project at derpydonut.com.
